What We Do
Kitchen & Bath
Milligan Remodeling is excited to serve Athens GA and surrounding Atlanta communities with kitchen and bathroom remodeling services. If you are thinking about spicing up your space and you’re not sure where to start, we will help you design your new kitchen or bathroom. If you already have a vision of how you want your new bathroom or kitchen to look, we will execute your design to make your dreams come true!
Deck Building
We build decks! Our locally owned and operated business in Athens GA is the community’s favorite deck building. New patios and decks can improve the curb appeal of your home or business, increase the value of your home or commercial property, and provide a lovely outdoor space for lounging and get-togethers.
Basements
If you want to finish your basement or remodel your already finished basement, Milligan Remodeling has you covered. Whatever you need, our crew is ready to complete your project including drywall installation, wall finishing, ceiling installation, floors, cabinets, fireplaces, duct work, staircases, and more. You name it and we got you!
Flooring
Milligan Remodeling provides floor installation services to commercial businesses and residential homeowners in Athens and the Atlanta area. Whatever flooring needs you have, Milligan Remodeling is ready to serve you and complete your project timely.
Siding Repair & Replacement
Milligan Remodeling has decades of experience with home and commercial exterior repair and maintenance. We specialize in repairing and installing vinyl and other type of siding. If you have noticed any warped or bent pieces of siding on your house or commercial facility, or you would like to refresh the look of your home, we are ready to help you get to the look you want.
